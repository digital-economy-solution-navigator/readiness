What is the Data Readiness Assessment?

The DRA provides a structured approach for Member States, CoEs, and technical cooperation projects to understand their readiness for AI-enabled solutions. It serves as a common diagnostic layer to support the design of CoE programmes, lighthouse projects, and sectoral digital transformation initiatives.

The framework is organized into two overarching folds ‚Äî Technical Readiness and Social Readiness ‚Äî each with three dimensions. Together, they create a holistic lens to evaluate whether data can meaningfully support digital transformation.
